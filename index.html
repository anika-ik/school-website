Vidyashilp Academy

Vidyashilp Academy school in bangalore

Vidyashilp Academy is a private, co-educational school located in Jakkur, Bangalore, India. Vidyashilp Academy is a top-ranking ICSE and Cambridge school located in Bangalore North. The school is also a member of the Association of International Schools in India. While it has its own in-house curriculum, the school also follows the ICSE board along with A Levels.
